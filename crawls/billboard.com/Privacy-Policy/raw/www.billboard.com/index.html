  • Katy and Carly Honored at Women in Music

    "I am not a feminist, but I do believe in the strength of women," Katy Perry said Friday (Nov. 30) during her "Woman of the Year" acceptance speech at Billboard's Women In Music 2012 luncheon. Also honored at the star-studded affair: Rising Star recipient Carly Rae Jepsen!

  • MJ's 'Thriller' at 30: Classic Track By Track

    As Michael Jackson's "Thriller" marks 30 years of pop perfection, we look and listen back on one of the most important albums of all time.

  • Katy Perry Q&A: Woman of the Year

    In a freewheeling interview, Katy Perry talks about her heroes, next album plans and what the last year has been like. "I'm glad I'm still alive -- 27 has been a very testing year."

  • Photos of the Week

    Alicia Keys sings on "X Factor," Cee Lo partied with the Muppets, Britney Spears showed off her new puppy, Justin Bieber wore overalls with the Canadian prime minister, Ke$ha hung out with Willie Nelson on NBC's "Today," and more. See the week's best pics!

  • Carly Rae Jepsen: Billboard's Rising Star

    Carly Rae Jepsen's wholly organic path to success -- marked by, but not defined by, the impossibly catchy song of the summer -- has earned her the 2012 Billboard Rising Star award.

  • Watch Lianne La Havas Sing Jill Scott

    In this new installment of Billboard's Women In Music-themed Candid Covers video series, rising star Lianne La Havas covers Jill Scott's "He Loves Me," a song from her childhood that had big influence on her musically.
