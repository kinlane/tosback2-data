usestealth.com ("Stealth") is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of its user community.


This privacy policy details the information Stealth may gather from the individuals who visit us, how that information is used, and our disclosure policy.


IP Addresses


The IP address that is associated with your search will NOT be recorded.

The only exception is for “abusive” (automated) queriers that rapidly submit many queries to Stealth in a very short time.


Data Collection


Stealth does not collect any information each time you visit our site, so there is no personalization at all in your online experience.

Absolutely NO personally identifiable data is collected.


Cookies


Cookies are small pieces of data that are sent and saved to your computer's hard drive when visiting sites.

No cookies are stored at all.


External Links


As a search engine you will find various links on our site, including but not limited to search results. These sites are owned and maintained by independent companies, over which Stealth has no control, and who are not covered by this privacy policy.

Stealth is not liable or responsible for the content of these sites, or their collection of information and its use. Please note that this privacy policy applies only to the usestealth.com website.


Children Under 13 Years of Age


In light of the concern over protecting children's privacy online, Stealth does not knowingly request, collect, or share personally identifiable information from users under the age of 13.


Information Sharing


Stealth does not share personal information with any third party search engine or the provider of its sponsored results. Stealth doesn't collect any personally identifiable information at all.


We will comply with court ordered legal requests. However, in our case, we don't expect any because there is nothing useful to give them since we don't collect any personal information.
