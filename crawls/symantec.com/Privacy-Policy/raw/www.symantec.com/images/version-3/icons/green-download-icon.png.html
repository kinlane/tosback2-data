Symantec Protection Engine for Cloud Services

Protect applications, services, and storage with fast, scalable threat detection and content control.

Symantec Protection Engine for Cloud Services 7.0 is a flexible and feature rich client/server application that allows customers to incorporate malware and threat detection technologies into almost any application. Protection Engine includes Symantec's proprietary, patented URL categorization technology and industry-leading malware protection for fast, scalable, and reliable content scanning services. These services help organizations protect their data and storage systems against the ever-growing malware threat landscape.
