Privacy Policy
Security/Privacy
We at mattressgiant.com respect your concern about privacy and we'd like you to know that we value our relationship with you. Our goal is to provide each mattressgiant.com guest with the best shopping experience possible. We take care to safeguard the information with which you entrust us. Please take a few minutes to read about how we collect, use and protect information about online guests of mattressgiant.com.
What information do we collect?
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and information volunteered by the consumer when placing an order.

The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page and to process orders placed by the consumer. We do not share any information collected with other organizations for commercial purposes.
What are "Cookies" and how do we use them?
A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer when you visit a web site. This file gives your computer a unique identification. mattressgiant.com uses session cookies to record information, such as items that you add to your shopping cart. These cookies exist only for as long as your browser remains open. After you exit your browser, these cookies go away.
What choices do I have regarding mattressgiant.com's use of information about me?

E-Mails from mattressgiant.com
You will only receive e-mail from mattressgiant.com in response to transactions you have made on our site. These may include e-mails to confirm orders or to relay information regarding orders you have placed at mattressgiant.com. You may receive e-mail in reply to questions, concerns or complaints you have previously contacted us about via mattressgiant.com. If you would prefer that we do not contact you via e-mail, please let us know by sending us e-mail at the above address.

Your address and telephone number
You are required to provide a delivery address and billing address only at the time of making a purchase from mattressgiant.com. You may receive periodic mailings from us. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending e-mail to the above address or by calling us at the above number. In addition, telephone numbers collected on mattressgiant.com are used only to contact our customers regarding orders they have placed on-line. You will not receive telephone calls regarding new products and services, upcoming events, etc.

From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future, we will use (for these new purposes only) data collected from the time of the policy change forward.
Our Commitment to Data Security
We have appropriate physical, electronic and procedural security safeguards to protect and secure the information we collect. We use industry-standard encryption technologies when transferring and receiving consumer data exchanged with our site.
Children
We are fully committed to protecting children's privacy. We recognize the particular importance of this issue and do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. We ask that a parent or guardian call 1-800-422-6823 or contact us if your child has provided mattressgiant.com with personally identifiable information and we will delete the information about the child from our files.
Your consent
By using the mattressgiant.com Website, you consent to our use of guest information as described in this Online Privacy Policy. We may change or add to this privacy policy, so we encourage you to review it periodically.
