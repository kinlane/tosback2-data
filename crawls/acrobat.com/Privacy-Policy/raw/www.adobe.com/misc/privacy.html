Adobe Privacy Center

To make our privacy policy easier to understand for all our users, Adobe revised and reorganized the information about how we collect and use your personal information. The most important improvements and changes we’ve made to our prior privacy policy are that we:

 

• Revised the language to make our privacy policy easier to understand

• Described our practices related to common privacy topics, such as interest-based advertising

• Created a single privacy policy that applies to the majority of our websites, products, online services, and apps

• Described how we measure your activities on our websites and online services to provide you with a more relevant experience based on those activities and other actions you take with your Adobe account (tell us not to use cookies for these purposes)

• Centralized the privacy choices we provide you

• Described privacy considerations related to Adobe hosted services
