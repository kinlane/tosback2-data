U-verse Receivers

DVR Receiver

The U-verse DVR receiver lets you record up to four shows at once on a single DVR and record and play back shows from any room in the house. Other great features of the DVR receiver include:

  • Schedule, update or delete your recordings from any TV in your home.
  • Pause, fast forward and rewind live or recorded shows on any TV in your home.
  • Play the same recorded show on different TVs at the same time and control them separately.
  • 100% digital audio and video – including Dolby Digital availability.
  • HD Enabled and Energy Star Certified.
  • Record up to 233 hours/SD or 65 hours/HD programming1.
  • Access the Guide, Menu, U-verse Movies, On Demand, PPV and manage Parental Controls.

Wireless Receiver

Enjoy TV virtually anywhere in your home with the Wireless Receiver. Exclusively from AT&T U-verse.

  • The U-verse wireless receiver gives you the freedom to place your receiver where you want, regardless of where your existing TV outlets are located.
  • Wireless signal strength indicator will help you position the receiver for optimal performance.
  • Access to Applications, Multiviews, Channel Guide, Menu, and the other features you use most.
  • Streams live HD & SD TV programming.
  • Functions like a standard receiver including: Total Home DVR®, U-verse Movies, and Pay-Per-View.
  • Installation is fast and easy.

Standard Receiver

The U-verse Standard Receiver supports Total Home DVR® features, when you order a qualifying package. The Standard Receiver also includes:

  • Full DVR capability including ability to pause live TV.
  • 100% digital audio and video – including Dolby Digital availability.
  • HD Enabled and Energy Star Certified.
  • Access the Guide, Menu, U-verse Movies, On Demand, and PPV.
  • Set Parental Controls.

Uverse Remotes

 

Standard Remote

Included with your U-verse order, the Standard Remote is a dynamic device that , with the push of a button, lets you have  instant one-button access to U-verse U-bar, Program Guide, On Demand, and DVR recordings. With the Standard Remote, you can:

  • Operate easily with backlighting to work well in low-light conditions.
  • Open Picture-In-Picture (PIP) browsing to look at other channels while not leaving the show you're watching.
  • Use the universal infrared to control up to 3 additional devices, like your TV, DVD player, or Home Theater.

Point Anywhere RF Remote

If you need to be able to access your U-verse TV from anywhere in your home, upgrade to the Point Anywhere RF Remote. The Point Anywhere RF Remote Control allows you to control your U-verse receiver through walls and eliminates the need to point directly at the receiver. The USB adaptor makes it easy to setup and it’s compatible with all U-verse TV receivers.

  • Control your U-verse receiver without line of sight - in a media closet or behind the closed doors of your entertainment center.
  • Simple plug-n-play USB adaptor - requires no power cords, or bulky antennas.
  • A single remote control that's fully programmable and learns from your existing remotes.
  • Backlit for use in low-light conditions.
  • One-button access to the U-verse TV features you use most, like U-verse U-bar, Program  Guide, On Demand, and DVR recordings.
  • Controls up to 4 devices like your TV, DVD player or Home Theater.
  • With 10,000 possible RF codes to select from, you will not encounter interference from other RF remotes.

Wireless Gateway

The AT&T U-verse Wireless Gateway is an intelligent device that brings together all your TV, Internet, and Voice U-verse services and distributes them throughout your home. The Wireless Gateway lets you watch TV while you:

  • Get personalized weather, sports, and stock information.
  • Share photos or stream music.
  • Initiate a phone call with your remote.

The Wireless Gateway enables wireless networking capabilities throughout your home and is powerful enough to help eliminate wireless dead spots. With encryption security, you can safeguard against outside access of your Internet connection. In addition, the Wireless Gateway provides Ethernet ports for wired LAN connections.

U-verse TV Installation

Professional standard installation3 is included with most plans.

Getting Ready

To get everything ready for your U-verse TV installation:

  • Check your appointment day and time and make sure it’s at a convenient time. If you need to reschedule, please try to make the change at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment time.
  • If you rent or lease your home, make sure you get the owner’s consent.
  • Make sure the paths to outlets and jacks are clear of furniture.
  • Only cancel your current cable TV service after U-verse is installed.
  • Plan for about 4 hours of installation time and make sure someone over 18 years of age will be there during the installation.

Connecting your High Definition (HD) Service

To enjoy U-verse in HD4, you'll need:

  • A high definition television (HDTV)
  • An HD receiver
  • HDMI cable, HDMI-to-DVI conversion with audio cable, or component
    cable for each receiver that will be connected to an HDTV

Setting up your HDTV

Your U-verse TV Order

To check the status of your order or your installation appointment, wait 24 hours after you place the order, then go to the Order Status Tool. It will give you information on installation date and time, technician arrival status, and shipping and tracking.

If you placed your order online, you'll receive emails from AT&T with information about your order confirmation, account number, and installation appointment time.

If your order is eligible for rewards or rebates, check the AT&T Rewards Center to claim them.

Installation

On the day of your U-verse Installation:

  • Your technician will call to give you an estimated time of arrival.
  • The technician will review your order to make sure everything is correct.
  • Basic testing will be performed to make sure U-verse can be installed at your address.
  • The technician will work with you to find the best place for your Wireless Gateway.
  • Installation is not complete until the technician makes sure that everything is working correctly.

To learn more about your U-verse Receiver, review the U-verse Receiver Manual.
