How to buy a wireless device as a gift

HOW TO GIVE A GIFT

We’re glad you like our toys

Found the perfect gift? Now what? Don’t worry. We can help.



NEW CUSTOMERS

Not an AT&T customer yet?

If you want to buy someone in your family a wireless device from AT&T but aren't a current AT&T wireless customer, you can go ahead and purchase a device online. Just remember that the service you set up will be in your name and you'll be responsible for the account.



CURRENT CUSTOMERS

Share the joy of AT&T

  • Add a line - Give someone in your family a new device while adding them to your account or family plan.
  • Use your upgrade - Are you eligible for a device upgrade? Use it to get a gift for someone special!
  • Buy a device at the no-commit price - Whether or not you're eligible for an upgrade, you can always buy a device at the no-commit price.

How to Give the Gift of Wireless

Giving a wireless gift can be easy. With just a few steps, you’re on your way to giving a wireless gift.

ADD A LINE

Give a gift by adding a line

If you're getting a gift for someone in your family, the easiest way
is to add a line to your account.

1.Log in to your account and choose Add a new individual plan
or Add a line to my FamilyTalk® plan.

2.Choose a device and an appropriate plan.

3.We'll ship you the device for free, and billing will begin as soon
as the device ships. Also, if you buy a phone in-store, you can have it
activated on a day of your choosing.

Log in to your account >













MORE INFO

Keep an existing number

If the person getting your gift already has a wireless number, we can help you transfer their existing number to their new device. Be ready with the following information about their account: the account holder’s name, account number, billing address, Social Security number or tax ID, and PIN/password for the account if they have one.


Transfer a number after purchase:
• Once the new device has been activated, you’ll have 30 days to transfer an existing number.

• Call the Porting Activation Center (PAC) at 888-898-7685 once you have the new device.

• Don’t cancel their current service! We’ll take care of it after the number has been ported.


Transfer a number online during purchase:
• You can transfer an existing number to the new device during the ordering process. Just enter the current account information when prompted.

• Note: Don't cancel their current phone service! We’ll take care of that when their new device is activated.

Learn more about porting an existing number >
UPGRADE

Give a gift by using your upgrade

You can use your own device upgrade to get a gift for someone
else at a great price.

1. Log in to your account to see if you're eligible for an upgrade.

2. Once you confirm that you're eligible, choose a device that you
want to give.

3. You should also order an AT&T SIM card to use with the new
device. Learn more about AT&T SIM cards.

4. Remember that by using your upgrade, you're agreeing to an
additional two-year commitment towards your own wireless service.

5. After purchase, the recipient will have 52 days to activate the
new device with their own information.

Log in to your account >








NO-COMMIT

Give a gift by buying at the no-commit price

You can always buy a device at no-commit pricing whether or not you're eligible for an upgrade.


1. Log in to your account so that you can shop for devices.

2. Choose the "no-commit" pricing for your device.

3. You should also order an AT&T SIM card to use with the new device. Learn more about AT&T SIM cards.

4. Once you have the device, you can activate it by visiting att.com/upatedevice or calling Customer Care at 800-331-0500.


Log in to your account >







TABLETS

Choose the plan that’s right for you

Get the tablet you want with the entertainment, productivity, and
social media you need. There are a few ways to buy a tablet for
you or someone else:

1. Share data for all your devices, plus get Unlimited Talk & Text
for your phones on one simple plan. AT&T Mobile Share
plans include a monthly data charge plus a monthly charge for each
device you add, for a maximum total of up to 10 devices. Add a
tablet for only $10 every month. Visit the Mobile Share page
for more information.

2. However, if you’re looking for a flexible, no long-term contract, and the
ability to cancel your plan at any time, then the AT&T Data Connect Pass
plan is a perfect fit. Visit the DataConnect Pass page for more information.
