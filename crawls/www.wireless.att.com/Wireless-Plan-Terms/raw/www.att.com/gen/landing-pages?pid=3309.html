Developer Taps into AT&T Network to Power App for Deaf & Hard of Hearing

January 18, 2013

With the power of AT&T’s Speech API, Kunal Batra recently launched Deaftel – a new phone service that enables the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community to converse in real time without the need for relay operators, converting voice into text.
